5-DAY WEEKLY PLAN
ABOUT YOUR PROGRAM

Your program is structured to progressively overload each week. Week 1 is back-safe and low impact. From Week 2 you'll add volume and difficulty.


Chest & Push — Mon & Thu. Push-ups are your primary chest builder. Variations get harder weekly.


Core — Tue & Fri. Planks, dead bugs, leg raises. No crunches (back-safe).


Active Recovery — Wed. Walk, bike, skate. Low heart rate. Keeps blood flowing.


Full Body + Cardio — Sat. Mixes all movement patterns. Jog/run optional.


Rest — Sun. Mandatory. Muscles grow on rest days, not workout days.
